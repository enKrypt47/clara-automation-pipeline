Let me go over our call handling rules.

Our office hours are Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM Pacific Time.

Emergency situations for us are mostly sprinkler pipe bursts or active fire alarms in a building.

If an emergency is reported, the system should collect the caller’s name, phone number, and building address right away before attempting to transfer the call.

After collecting those details, the call should be transferred to our dispatch number.

If the transfer fails, the caller should be reassured that a technician will contact them shortly.
